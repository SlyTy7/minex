About Us

At Minex Solutions our main goal is to make mining cryptocurrencies as easy as possible. We host the equipment and do the mining for you for a small monthly hosting fee subtracted from the amount of coin we've mined for you. You don’t have to worry about making monthly payments, we setup your rig to mine directly to your wallet, on your behalf! We simply mine part-time for ourselves to cover our management and hosting fee. The rest of the time that we are mining, we deposit those funds directly to your wallet address.

How It Works

You determine how much money you’d like to invest in your mining operation. This is a onetime fee. You purchase the equipment up front. You own the equipment. You can have us ship it to you at any time. After the initial purchase we setup the equipment to mine for you, to your wallet. You can sit back and let us do the work.

In addition to the one time fee for purchasing the equipment we take a small monthly hosting fee. This hosting fee covers our costs for hosting and managing the equipment for you. Some of these costs include:

Electricity

The single greatest cost in mining. Residential electric can be expensive. If you’re mining from your home it is not uncommon to spend 25-35% of your total monthly earnings just paying your electricity.

Heating/Cooling

Cryptographic puzzles are very intense. So, in turn, mining will heat up the components pretty fast. So you need proper temperature control and heat dissipation when operating a miner.

Internet

Mining consumes a lot of bandwidth, more than the average house-hold consumes in a month!

Insurance

All our Locations are covered with insurance, meaning if anything was to happen to your equipment, this would be covered.

Building Rent

We rent spaces in secure data centres across the UK.

Customer Support

24/7 support! We also have created a portal so you can watch over all your mined crypto’s, including Temperature, Bandwidth and power consumption.

Maintenance

It doesn’t matter whether you have an ASIC based mining rig or GPU based, it needs maintenance. Or else it will all go in vain if you have to change your rig’s components after a year. Preferably you want your components to last at least 3 years. Having components that last at least three years ensures that you get good profit.
